Guides for Seniors


Here is a list of the guides we have published for seniors. We are trying to cover as many topics as we can to educate seniors on how to use the Internet. Our goal at the moment is to publish one comprehensive guide per fortnight. If you would like to be informed when we publish these, please enter your email address on the right, we also gladly welcome topic suggestions.


Seniors' Guide to Instagram


This guide takes a step-by-step approach to introduce you to what Instagram is, how you can use it to view the photos of your family and friends, how you can share your photos with family and friends. What filters are, what "likes" are, what "hashtags" are, etc. If you didn't understand this description then this guide is made for you. Click here to read it.




The Seniors' Guide to YouTube - Part One


Our YouTube guide for seniors covers everything that you will need to know about how to use YouTube. Click here to read the YouTube guide.



How do I get a seniors card?


This comprehensive guide shows you how to get a seniors card anywhere in Australia. It includes online seniors card application, downloadable application form, eligibility requirements, addresses and phone numbers. Click here to read the guide. 



Massage for seniors: a guide to the benefits of massage for seniors


This guide tells you what you need to know about massage for seniors. The benefits of massage therapy for seniors, what a seniors massage entails and the types available. Click here to read the massage for seniors guide.



The seniors' guide to international plane travel - flying and airports


All about travelling internationally. Getting to the airport, checking in, customers/security, at the airport, boarding, on the plane and what to do when you arrive and more. Read the guide to seniors plane travel.



The seniors travel guide to Australia Part 1 - NSW


In this first of series guide we start to talk about travel for seniors in our own backyard - NSW. We cover places to go, unique and interesting things to do, travel tips, and links to help you find activities and accommodation.

Click here to read the seniors travel guide to Australia in NSW.



How do I get back here? A guide to bookmarking websites


This guide shows you the ins and outs of creating "bookmarks" of your favourite websites so that you never lose them. It covers all of the major web browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari and Internet Explorer. 

Click here to read the guide.
